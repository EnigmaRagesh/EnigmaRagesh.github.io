about me

Hello. I am Ragesh. I am a Roboticist with work experience ranging from Planetary rovers to industrial manipulators. Robotics is my passion and I'm keen about building robots and writing software to bring them to life.

I love participating in robotics competitions and conferences because for me it's the only way to stay updated in this field. I have participated in and won prizes in numerous international robotic challenges. I see robotics as my hobby rather than a profession, So I never feel tired or afraid while trying something new.

Being involved in numerous projects right from the conceptual stage to their commissioning, I have acquired extensive problem-solving capabilities in the domain of autonomous robotics. I am rooted with a strong academic background of robotics theory along with the applied software and hardware experience.

Apart from my interest in robotics, I am a backpacker, shutterbug, and a movie freak. I spend my free time editing photos and planning for more trips. I have traveled around many countries in Europe and I am still in the pursuit of visiting more places around the world.

My long term goal is to make use of robotics and Artificial Intelligence along with sustainable energy sources in the field of agriculture because I am very much concerned about climate change and I feel that even the smallest contribution helps.
