Hello, I’m Ragesh Ramachandran. I’m a Roboticist, having an experience of work ranging from planetary rovers to industrial manipulators. For me, robotics has been my fervor, and I’m keen on building robots and writing software to bring them into reality.

I’m very zealous to participate in all competitions and conferences regarding robotics, and that is how I keep myself updated in the discipline. I have been to numerous international robotics challenges and always stood up as a winner. I consider robotics more than a profession, which often leads me to try something new.

Incorporating myself in quite a few projects, right from its conceptual stage to bring it in action, I have acquired extensive problem-solving skills in the domain of autonomous robotics. I am provided with a strong academic background of robotics theory along with the applied software and hardware experience.

Apart from my gusto in robotics, I’m a backpacker, shutterbug, and a movie buff. I have been to many countries in Europe. Also, I’m still in the pursuit of visiting more places around the world.

My long term goal is to make use of robotics and artificial intelligence along with sustainable energy sources in the field of agriculture because I’m very much concerned about climate change and feel that even the smallest contribution helps.
